Pikachu's Family Through The Generations.

Pikachu started off fairly simple in the begenning. At first, there was only Pikachu and Raichu in the family tree. As the generations of Pokemon went on, this family tree started to grow, and a pre-evolution was added (mentioned earlier - Pichu). This is a list of the generations from Gen 1 to Gen 8.

  1. Pikachu, Raichu
  2. Pichu
  3. Plusle, Minun
  4. Pachirisu
  5. Emolga
  6. Dedenne
  7. Togedemaru, Alolan Raichu, Partner Pikachu
  8. Morpeko, Gigantamax Pikachu
